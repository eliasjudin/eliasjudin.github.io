#category-theory

(Last updated: December 2022)

I will be making a series of posts that contains edited versions of some of the short answers I submitted for my category theory course at the University of Cape Town. These were answers to a number of exercises in Prof. George Janelidze’s category theory notes1. Every week we had to submit a short essay of sorts, which made up our continuos assessment mark, as well as a final essay which functioned as our exam.

Here are the links to the posts (as they are uploaded):

  • Groupoids and skeletons of monoids and preorders
  • Free algebras by means of a universal property
  • Category of modules and category of vector spaces as algebraic categories
  • M-sets and Yoneda for monoids
  • hom-Sets
  • Monomorphisms and epimorphisms

  1. George Janelidze, Category theory: A first course, 2020. ↩︎
